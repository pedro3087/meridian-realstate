Feature Face-Off

Maintenance & Your Time

How much hands-on work are you ready for? Self-hosting means you are the system administrator. The Cloud plan lets you focus purely on workflows.

Control vs. Convenience

This is the core trade-off. Self-hosting offers unparalleled control over your environment, while the cloud subscription provides maximum ease of use.

Workflow Executions

Self-hosting provides unlimited executions, bound only by your server's power. Cloud plans have defined limits, which is often plenty for many use cases.
